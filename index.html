Greetings & Politeness

  • Hello: Konnichiwa  🔊

  • Good morning: Ohayou gozaimasu 🔊

  • Good evening: Konbanwa  🔊

  • Goodbye: Sayounara  🔊

  • Thank you: Arigatou gozaimasu  🔊

  • Thank you (more polite): Doumo arigatou gozaimasu  🔊

  • Excuse me / Sorry: Sumimasen  🔊

  • Excuse me (to get attention): Sumimasen, onegaishimasu  🔊

  • Yes: Hai  🔊

  • No: Iie  🔊 

  • No, thank you: Iie, kekkou desu  🔊 

  • Please (request): Onegaishimasu  🔊

Directions & Travel

  • Where is...?: Doko desu ka? 🔊

  • Station: Eki  🔊

  • Airport: Kuukou  🔊

  • Hotel: Hoteru  🔊

  • Bathroom: Toire  🔊

  • Left: Hidari 🔊

  • Right: Migi  🔊

  • Straight ahead: Massugu 🔊

Dining

  • Restaurant: Resutoran (レストラン) 🔊

  • Water: Mizu 🔊

  • Menu, please: Menyuu o kudasai 🔊

  • Delicious: Oishii 🔊

  • I am allergic to...: ～ ni arerugii ga arimasu 🔊

  • Check, please: Okaikei onegaishimasu 🔊

Shopping

  • How much is this?: Kore wa ikura desu ka? 🔊

  • I would like this: Kore o kudasai 🔊

  • Too expensive: Takasugimasu 🔊

Emergency

  • Help me!: Tasukete! (助けて!) 🔊

  • Help!: Herupu! (ヘルプ!) 🔊

  • Police: Keisatsu (警察)  🔊

  • Hospital: Byouin (病院) 🔊

  • I need a doctor: Isha ga hitsuyou desu 🔊

  • Pharmacy: Yakkyoku ( 薬局) 🔊

Transportation

  • Bus stop: バス停 (Basu tei)  🔊
  • Train station: 駅 (Eki)  🔊
  • Ticket: 切符 (Kippu) 🔊
  • Timetable: 時刻表 (Jikokuhyou) 🔊

General Signs

  • Push: 押す (Osu) 🔊
  • Pull: 引く (Hiku) 🔊
  • Entrance: 入口 (Iriguchi) 🔊
  • Exit: 出口 (Deguchi) 🔊
  • Open: 開ける (Akeru) 🔊
  • Closed: 閉まる (Shimaru) 🔊
  • Emergency exit: 非常口 (Hijouguchi) 🔊
